Free and Open Source Software

For Game Development

Website by Jasen Harper

Free and open source software are some of the best software for starting out in the game design and development industry. Since these types of software are free to use as well as customizable, developers can worry more about how they are going to make their game, rather than what they are going to make their game with.

Through the use of open source software, game developers in both larg and small companies can help to create tools that they want and need to use. Whether it be due to lack of monetary resources or lack of essential tools, free and open source software are capable of delivering exactly what most any developer needs.
