Blender

Blender is a free software that functions primarily as a 3D modeling, sculpting, and animation tool. Many of Blender's features have come from its community, such as its new 2D animation tools.

Though it has a high learning curve comparatively to other programs like Autodesk Maya, its versatility makes up for this. While many of these functions will only be used by small portions of the community, at least that small portion is able to create everything that they want with one program.
