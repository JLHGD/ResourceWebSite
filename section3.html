Godot

Godot is an open source game engine that focusses on being a light-weight, easy to use alternative to other game engines, while still maintaining much of the functionality of other more commonly used game engines.

Being a light-weight engine, Godot does not support as polished functionality as other game engines, especially in 3D. Unity and the Unreal engines, for example, have better 3D graphics rendering and 3D physics capabilities. This does not mean that Godot can not create good 3D games, this just means that they will not be on par with AAA titles right now. Otherwise, Godot is a free and easy to use alternative with more or less the right amount of functionality for beginners and for those looking to create a game quickly.
