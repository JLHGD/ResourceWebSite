Krita

Krita is an open source digital painting tool with fully functional brush simulation, animation, and some image editing capabilities. Krita's community continues to refine and expand Krita's tool set to match what artists require to create digital paintings.

While Krita does ot have the image editing capabilities of programs like Adobe Photoshop, it does have painting tools comparable to those in Corel Painter and other professional painting programs. However, Krita's community is working on making Krita's image editing capabilities on par with Photoshop's.
